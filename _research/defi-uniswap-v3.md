abstract: "We document the emergence of a volatile winner-take-most equilibrium in Uniswap v3 following the introduction of a 0.01% fee tier for WETH/USDC in November 2022. By October 2025 the new tier captures a peak of 78% of trading volume and over 80% of liquidity activity. Informed and predatory order flow migrate disproportionately, generating 4x-7x higher absolute price impact and repeated loss-versus-rebalancing (LVR) spikes above 6x. From mid-2024, however, sophisticated liquidity providers adapt through hyper-active, ultra-narrow ranges and near-continuous rebalancing, driving the per-swap LVR ratio sharply downward. 	A single Uniswap v3 pool now contributes 67 % of Ethereum price discovery when compared with Binance (and the incumbent 0.30% fee tier). Placebo pools without lower-fee competitors exhibit no such shift."

journal: "International Journal of Financial Markets and Derivatives 8 (1), 23-49"
doi: "https://doi.org/10.1504/IJFMD.2021.114782"
abstract: "Investigates the role of liquidity in a limit order market using high-resolution data. Liquidity is modeled as both a store and a flow. Using a sample of NASDAQ 100 stocks, we find that liquidity residing in the order book and the flow from the latent order book can explain the wealth traded when observed in business time. The explanatory power is strong in high resolution but weakens in aggregated longer time periods."

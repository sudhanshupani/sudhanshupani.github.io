abstract: "Examines the dependence between liquidity, durations, and bid-ask spreads in high resolution using copulas and graph theory. Using NASDAQ 100 data, we study information pathways in the presence of algorithmic traders. Results confirm that multivariate analysis is more appropriate and identify stylized aspects of algorithmic trading like tail dependence."

About Me

I am an Assistant Professor of Finance and Area Chairperson of Finance and Accounting at School of Business Management, NMIMS Mumbai. My research focuses on market microstructure, consumer finance, and capital markets.

I hold a PhD from NMIMS University, an MBA from IIM Indore (first rank, Certificate of Merit), and a degree in Metallurgical and Materials Engineering from Utkal University. Before academia, I spent 15 years in banking with ICICI Bank, HDFC Bank, and Standard Chartered Bank, and founded Finance Central.

My research has appeared in Borsa Istanbul Review, Algorithmic Finance, Journal of Quantitative Economics, and other journals. I have authored books including The Whole: Systems Thinking for Business Strategy and Design (2025), Consumer Finance – Theory, Behaviour & Product Design (2024) and The Right Cocktail: Asset Allocation and Wealth Management in India (2016).

I teach Consumer Finance, Corporate Finance, and Investment Analysis, and conduct executive education for ICICI Bank, Asian Paints, Glenmark, L&T etc.
